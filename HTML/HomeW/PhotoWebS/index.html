About me

I'm Oksana. I call myself "" because I have no professional art education. However, the world is full of miracles..! and I have a great inner desire to capture it on canvas in oil.

For more than 10 years I have been involved in interior design and 3D visualization. But now, for the last 20 years, I have been captivated by painting.

The greatest impression on me was the work of Vincent Van Gogh, Claude Monet and Georges Seurat. Therefore, I mainly paint my paintings in the style of impressionism with elements of pointillism.

I will be glad to present you my works created during this time and in the future I will post all my new paintings. Let these little bears delight you with their ridiculous actions. If you are interested in my work, I will be glad to see you on my page!
